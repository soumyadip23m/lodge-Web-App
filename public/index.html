About New BayView

New Bay View Guest House is designed to make you feel at home. With spacious, air-conditioned rooms, private balconies, and a tranquil atmosphere, it's the ideal spot to unwind after a long day. Our friendly staff ensures a warm welcome and attentive service throughout your stay.

We’re proud to offer an on-site food court serving a variety of delicious meals to satisfy every palate. Whether you're craving local flavors, our food court is open to guests for breakfast, lunch, and dinner — all in a cozy, relaxed setting.
